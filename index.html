Kenny Torrella is a writer and animal advocate. His creative and critical writing can be found in The Rumpus, NAILED, Oblong, and Word Riot.

He's published dozens of op-eds and letters to the editor in support of farm animal protection issues, some of which have been printed in the Los Angeles Times, the Wall Street Journal, the Chicago Tribune, and the San Francisco Chronicle.

Additionally, he's played a key role in legislative campaigns for animals that have been featured in The Atlantic, the Boston Globe, the Washington Post, Quartz, and more.

Kenny lives in Washington, D.C. with his partner and their wonderful dog Rihana.
