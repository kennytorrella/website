Kenny Torrella is a writer and animal advocate in Washington, D.C.

By day, he serves as the Farm Animal Policy Outreach Manager at The Humane Society of the United States. He's played a key role in legislative campaigns for farm animal protection that have been featured in The Atlantic, the Boston Globe, the Washington Post, Quartz, and more, and has spoken at national and regional conferences on the topic. His writing on animal advocacy has been featured in The Accidental Activist.

By night, he writes short fiction and book reviews. His creative and critical writing can be found in The Rumpus, NAILED, Oblong, Word Riot, The Tennessee Literary Project and more.
